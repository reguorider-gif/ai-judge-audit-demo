1. Why This Report Exists

Most evaluation stacks can tell a team whether one output passed a metric. Agent teams also need to know whether the loop that produced it is safe to run again.

AI Judge is designed as a post-run Loop QA layer. It does not replace Ragas, Promptfoo, Langfuse, DeepEval, Phoenix, Opik, LangSmith, TruLens, or a coding agent. It sits after a model answer, RAG answer, trace, PR, support reply, or evaluator score and turns the run into an inspectable review record.

If an agent loop says an answer is faithful, complete, or safe, can a human reviewer inspect the path from trigger to evidence to verdict to next-run decision?

2. Sample Scenario

Loop Context

Trigger: a customer asks whether enterprise refunds, priority support, and beta features are guaranteed.

Handoff: a support agent retrieves policy sources, drafts a reply, and sends it to an evaluator before customer delivery.

Human gate: enterprise-policy answers require reviewer signoff before the loop is allowed to reply automatically.

User Task

Evaluate whether a customer-support agent reply is grounded in the provided policy sources, and decide whether this class of loop can continue automatically.

Source Packet

Source A: Refund Policy, updated 2026-05-04

  • Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
  • Digital subscriptions are refundable only if fewer than 10% of monthly credits have been used.
  • Enterprise contracts follow the signed master service agreement.

Source B: Support SLA, updated 2026-04-12

  • Standard support response target: 2 business days.
  • Priority support response target: 4 business hours.
  • SLA targets are not guaranteed during scheduled maintenance windows.

Source C: Product Terms, updated 2026-03-29

  • Beta features are provided as-is.
  • Customers may export account data from the dashboard.
  • Account deletion requests are completed within 14 days.

Candidate Agent Reply

You can get a refund within 30 days, but only if you have used fewer than 10% of your monthly credits. Enterprise customers always follow the same policy. Priority support responds within 4 hours, and the company guarantees this response time even during maintenance. Beta features are fully supported. You can export your data from the dashboard, and deletion requests are usually completed within 14 days.

5. Final Loop QA Verdict

Verdict: Hold automatic replies for this class of ticket until a human reviewer approves the rewrite.

Reason: The answer contains several correct source-grounded statements, but it converts exceptions and scope limits into broad guarantees. This is dangerous in customer support because incorrect statements sound like policy commitments, and the same loop could repeat the mistake at scale.

Safer Rewrite

You may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. For digital subscriptions, refunds are available only if fewer than 10% of monthly credits have been used. Enterprise customers should refer to their signed master service agreement. Priority support has a 4-business-hour response target, but SLA targets are not guaranteed during scheduled maintenance. Beta features are provided as-is. You can export account data from the dashboard, and account deletion requests are completed within 14 days.
