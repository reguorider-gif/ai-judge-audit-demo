Workflow

Designed to attach after the eval stack you already use.

AI Judge is not positioned as a replacement for Ragas, Promptfoo, DeepEval, Phoenix, Opik, LangSmith, Braintrust, or TruLens. It is the review layer for cases where a score alone is not enough.

1

Import

Bring a model answer, trace, eval result, rubric, and source packet.

2

Isolate

Split long output into atomic claims and bind each claim to explicit evidence.

3

Judge

Run independent judge seats and summarize agreement, dissent, and failure mode.

4

Sign off

Export a human-readable packet for review, regression, or customer-facing audit.
